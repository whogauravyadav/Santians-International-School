Our Best Facilities

We cater the Best Facilities

The motto of the school is to groom the future leaders. To facilitate this the school provides adequate facilities to the students to learn, socialise and thrive.

Library

The school is equipped with rich digitalised library facilitating fiction, non fiction and academic reading resources. The library is upgraded every year and possesses the collection of over 15000 titles. In addition, e-reading is supported by Kindles.

Information Technology

To keep updated with the advances in tech world, we promote the world's best practices for Curriculum delivery & Assessments which improves the educational outcome of all students.

Laboratories

To facilitate hand on skills, Language, Science and Computer labs are well established with modern and latest features.

Sports

“A healthy mind dwells in a healthy body”. Keeping this in mind the school promotes and encourages a number of sports activities.

Medical Care

A Health Centre with first aid facilities is available in school. For special medical care and emergancy services the school is associated with Santokba Durlabhji Memorial Hospital, Jaipur.
